Scheme Events

Scheme Workshop

The annual Scheme and Functional Programming Workshop (usually shortened to Scheme Workshop) is the most prominent Scheme event. It has been held every year since 2000, often alongside another conference about Lisp, functional programming, or dynamic languages. Since 2015, its host has been the International Conference on Functional Programming (ICFP).
